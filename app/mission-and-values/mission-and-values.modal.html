Tsawwassen Tennis Club Mission & Values

TTC Mission

“To be the friendliest small tennis club in Metro Vancouver”

TTC Executive Values & Behaviours

The Executive exists as the Steward of the Members’ needs. It will make prudent decisions that protect the finances of the club balanced with programs, activities, and facilities that the members desire.
The Executive will be the Champions of the club’s mission to be “The friendliest small tennis club in Metro Vancouver” and will model and reinforce the member behaviours and values.

Member Values & Behaviours

Members will behave in a way that maintains the club’s mission of being the friendliest small tennis club in Vancouver. Members will:

  • Welcome non-members who are investigating the club. Ask if they need any information or advice.
  • Respect the court allocations times for certain pre-organized events such as league and drop-in and make way to those players on the assigned courts.
  • Accept that members are at different levels of tennis abilities. Treat all other members with respect regardless of their tennis ability, recognizing that no one intentionally tries to make errors.
  • Recognize that the club cannot exist without the work of Volunteers, so take your turn accepting duties/responsibilities from time to time.
